FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to be based somewhere specific to work with you?
No. Mule works with clients globally. All communication happens via video calls, email, and shared project tools. Time zones are manageable — we make it work.
How long does a website take to build?
A typical Starter Presence takes 2–3 weeks. A full Brand + Web Package takes 4–6 weeks. Timelines depend on content readiness and revision rounds.
Do I need to be on a monthly plan?
No. Monthly plans are entirely optional. Many clients do a one-time project and manage things themselves afterward. We're happy either way.
What if I just need one small thing fixed?
Reach out. We don't require contracts for small jobs. If we can help quickly, we will. If the scope is larger, we'll recommend the right package.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my monthly plan?
Yes, anytime. We operate on month-to-month agreements. Upgrade when you're ready to grow, scale back when you need to — no penalties.
What do you need from me to get started?
Mostly your time and trust. A discovery call, any existing brand assets you have (logos, colours, photos), and your thoughts on what you love and hate about your current presence. We handle the rest.

Ready to be heard?

Tell us about your business. We'll put together a proposal and get on a call within 48 hours.
